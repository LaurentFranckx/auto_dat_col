Responses to reports by the Independent Chief Inspector of Borders and Immigration

From:
Home Office, Border Force and UK Visas and Immigration
First published:
27 November 2013

Home Office responses to inspection reports by John Vine.

Contents

  1. 2014
  2. 2013
  3. 2012

Official responses by the Home Office (including UK Visas and Immigation, Border Force and Immigration Enforcement) to reports by John Vine, the Independent Chief Inspector of Borders and Immigration.

The inspection reports can be found on the inspector’s website.

On 3 April 2014, the independent chief inspector released a batch of spot check reports. This batch covers the Home Office’s handling of complaints and MPs’ correspondence (carried out in September 2013), alongside entry clearance in Abu Dhabi and Islamabad (November 2013). No formal response is expected to spot checks.
